Formula Reference

Account Risk (₹)= Account Size × Risk %
Trade Risk (₹ / share)= Entry − Stop Loss
Ideal Qty (formula)= Account Risk ÷ Trade Risk
Max Affordable Qty= FLOOR( Account Size ÷ Entry Price )
Capped Qty= MIN( Ideal Qty , Max Affordable Qty )
% Profit on Capital= ( Qty × ( Target − Entry ) ) ÷ Capital Deployed × 100
% Loss on Account= ( Qty × Trade Risk ) ÷ Account Size × 100
